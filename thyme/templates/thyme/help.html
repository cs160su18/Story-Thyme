FAQ

How do I view timelines (thymelines) I've added recipes to?

Go to user profile by clicking the person icon in the top left of the Home or Search Results page. Tapping on "My Thymelines" should lead you to a page listing all timelines you have contributed to!

How does the timeline system work in general?

Timelines are organized by family. The "Smith Apple Pie Timeline", for example, would most likely contain recipes spanning several generations, from the great-grandmother to the grandmother to your current recipe. These recipes are organized by the rough date around which they were created, and you view the "story" of a family recipe when you view its timeline. The story of a family recipe is comprised of all the events that impacted recipes and what changed in them.

E.g. perhaps grandma decided to add brown sugar to the crust of the pie for the first time, or perhaps you decided to substitute coconut oil for butter in order to make the recipe more healthy, or perhaps your family immigrated to America fifty years ago, causing the family recipe to resemble American apple pie more than the original Dutch version. Tapping on the specific story that interests you will take you to its recipe.
